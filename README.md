Backend Developer
-----------------

👋 Hi there! I'm a mechanical engineering graduate turned software developer with 5+ years of professional experience in backend development and system architecture. I specialize in Python, FastAPI, PostgreSQL, and have hands-on experience with tools and technologies like Redis, Docker, Linux, and Git. My work has focused heavily on building scalable backend systems, database architecture, and workflow automation in high-throughput environments. In one of my major projects, I helped build and maintain a Warehouse Management System (WMS) backend that integrated with client APIs and robotics systems, supported real-time sorting logic, and enabled hardware strategy switching with zero downtime. I also developed simulation services for robotic testing and ensured a consistent backend interface across multiple frontends. Beyond the code, I’m interested in open-source communities (like Frappe/ERPNext) and exploring how my backend and systems experience can be applied to areas like climate science, geoinformatics, and environmental systems.
